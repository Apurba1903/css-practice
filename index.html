My name is Stewie Griffin

Hey there, folks! Stewie Griffin here, the prodigious and diabolically brilliant baby! Prepare to be astounded by my razor-sharp wit, unyielding sarcasm, and an insatiable thirst for world domination. Yes, I do believe it's time to conquer this puny planet, one sardonic comment at a time. So, grab your popcorn and brace yourselves for a hilariously twisted adventure through the mind of yours truly. Oh, and remember, I may be small in stature, but my intellect knows no bounds!
